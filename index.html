ABOUT US

We aim to help empower local non-profit groups to get the most out of their quantitative material by connecting them with motivated individuals experienced with complex data analysis, programming and visual analytics.

Local Organizations

Social and Environmental NGOs looking for help with data analysis, customized plots and GIS

Open Repositories

Allowing for accessible figures, content and supporting material, along with version control (github.com/communityplots)

Data Enthusiats

Individuals with experience in data analysis, programming and GIS looking to volunteer with community groups
